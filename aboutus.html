IMEQSAN BAND is a riffian groupe founded in 2015 in Tetouan by a group of college students, and after that it continued in Al Hoceima and participated in a series of musical events in the same city and other cities, such as Oujda, Rabat, Casablanca, Tetouan, Tangier ...
The band's main objective is to serve the Amazigh and riffian music in particular.
The band plays multiple styles by mixing the traditional and modern music to give birth to
it's particular style.
